Sailing and Snorkeling

$79.00 per adult
A small-group tour limited to 16 people sets this catamaran tour apart from its competitors. Set sail on a premium ship equipped with shareable net hammocks en route to a snorkeling stop at Cabeza de Toro Natural Reserve and a sandbar. Plus, unlimited drinks from an open bar, round-trip transport, and use of snorkel equipment make it easy to vacation without handling the stressful details of the day. Duration:3-4 hours.Visit Tripadvisor!

Horseback Riding

$60.00 per adult
This horseback riding tour features the sweeping landscapes of Uvero Alto Beach. It’s one of Punta Cana’s most sought-after coastlines, far from the crowded resorts. Guides provide instruction and accompany you throughout the ride. Afterward, take home a memorable souvenir: a local rum bottle with your group photo on it. Hotel transport included. Duration:2 hours.Visit Tripadvisor!

Rivers and Caves

$60.52 per adult
Combine several different adventure activities on a single half-day tour from Punta Cana. Hop on board a powerful all-terrain vehicle, built for the rugged terrain of the Dominican Republic's interior, riding through a forest to a local organic farm. Taste coffee, chocolate, and rum before exploring a local cave and underground river. End up at Macao Beach for a chance to relax and swim before returning to Punta Cana. Duration:4 hours. Visit Tripadvisor!
